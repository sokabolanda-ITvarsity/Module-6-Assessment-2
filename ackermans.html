ACKERMANS

The Ackermans story goes back as far as 1916, when Gus Ackerman opened the first store in Wynberg in Cape Town. His vision for the store was to create a place where customers could find great products at fantastic prices, which is exactly what Ackermans became. Ackermans was, subsequently, bought by the Pepkor Group in 1986.

Today, there are more than 450 Ackermans stores in 5 countries. With a wide selection of fashion for ladies, kids, babies and men, as well as homeware, cellular and key financial products. We also do this by providing you with useful services and benefits. For example, our A.Rewards loyalty programme makes shopping even more rewarding when you swipe, by awarding you with Stars with every purchase. These Stars are converted into cash-back vouchers that can be used in-store!
