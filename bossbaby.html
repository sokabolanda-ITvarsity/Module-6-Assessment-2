THE BOSS BABY: FAMILY BUSINESS

Director: Tom McGrath

Producer: Jeff Hermann

Writer: Michael McCullers

Runtime: 107 minutes

Cast: Alec Baldwin, James Marsden, Amy Sedaris, Ariana Greenblatt, Jeff Goldblum, Eva Longoria, Jimmy Kimmel, Lisa Kudrow

Synopsis: Now adults, Tim Templeton is a stay-at-home dad for two adorable daughters, while his estranged brother, Ted, is a big-shot CEO.
