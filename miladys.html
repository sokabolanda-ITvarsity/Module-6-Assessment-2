MILDAYS

We believe every great success story starts with a woman. Well, ours started with two. In 1947, Mrs Bailey and Mrs Elison opened a dress shop in Pinetown. And so Miladys was born. At Miladys, we think women are fabulous.

We think you deserve to look your best – and feel great while doing it. We walk the line between fashion and comfort. We believe a good day starts with a good outfit. We are passionate about beautiful, well-made dresses (and denims and tops and skirts). We know there’s more than one way to wear an outfit – and we love clothes you can wear again and again. We also know that sometimes all you need is a good tee – and we’ve got those too. We think clothes should work for your shape, not against it. What we’re really trying to say is, we get women.
